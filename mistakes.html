Mistakes

How teams accidentally make OpenClaw worse than it needs to be

Avoidable failure modes with practical, operator-grade fixes.

Too much autonomy too soon

Fix:

Increase autonomy only after repeatable quality at lower control levels.

Missing workflow owners

Fix:

Assign a named human owner for every lane and every decision queue.

No standard reporting

Fix:

Use one reporting template for every worker output to preserve comparability.

Integration before process clarity

Fix:

Stabilize workflow logic first, then automate and integrate step by step.
